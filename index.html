About

I'm an architecture student from Tamil Nadu working at the intersection of climate-responsive design, structural efficiency, and regional cultural identity. My recent work spans a Mivan-construction high-density residential complex, a steel exhibition pavilion, and live pre-construction commissions in hospitality and residential typologies.

I presented my GRIHA Trophy-shortlisted project, Monolithic Efficiency, at the 68th NASA Annual Convention at Woxsen University, Hyderabad — validated through IES-VE climate simulation. I also lead peer workshops on using AI as a design communication tool, not a design generator.
